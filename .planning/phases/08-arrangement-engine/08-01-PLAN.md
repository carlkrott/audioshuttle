### Task 3: Rewrite `_generate_instrument_pattern()` → `_generate_arrangement()`
**Action:** Replace the method. New signature:
```python
def _generate_arrangement(self, midi, mtrack, role, scale_notes, sections, bpm)
```
- Iterates sections, looks up profile, calls `_generate_section_pattern()` per section
- Uses `random.seed(hash(role + section_name + key))` for reproducible variation
- Places notes at `start_bar * 4` beat offset per section

### Task 4: Implement `_generate_section_pattern()`
**Action:** New method that generates MIDI for one instrument in one section
```python
def _generate_section_pattern(self, midi, mtrack, role, scale_notes,
                               start_bar, bars, density, vel_range, section_type, bpm)
```
Role-specific logic:
- **Drums:** Density controls hat subdivision (quarters→eighths→sixteenths). Chorus gets "four on the floor". Ghost notes above density 0.7.
- **Bass:** Root-fifth pattern at density 0.3-0.5. Walking pattern at 0.6+. Octave jumps at 0.9+.
- **Melody/Lead:** Scale-walking at low density. Wider intervals at high density. Rests at density < 0.4.
- **Keys/Chords:** Whole-note block chords at low density. Arpeggiated at high density. I-IV-V at low energy, ii-V-I at high energy.
- **Pad:** Long sustained chords. More notes added at higher density.
- **Strings:** Sustained harmony. Counter-melody fragments at high density.
- **Arp:** Fast scale runs, speed scales with density.
- **FX Riser:** Ascending pitch sweep, duration proportional to bars.

**Verification:** Unit test per role verifying velocity ranges, density behavior, scale confinement
